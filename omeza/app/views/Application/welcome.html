Welcome to Omeza.org

Are you trying to get pregnant, or looking for a natural way of contraception?

Omeza.org is your tool for monitoring your Basal Body Temperature and your menstrual cycle. The goal is to know better your body to estimate your days of fertility; this can be used to increase your chances of getting pregnant.

What is Basal Body Temperature?

Basal body temperature is the temperature measured on morning, right after waking up. Ovulation can cause an increase of one quarter to one half degree celcius of temperature, so measuring your temperature every day can help you detect ovulation thus fertility time.

What do I need to track my basal body temperature?

Classical thermometers are not sensitive enough to measure BBT. You need a basal thermometer: those are accurate to 0.05C or 0.1F, which is necessary for BBT measurements.
